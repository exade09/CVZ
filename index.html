About

Cat Vs Zomb is a meme coin born from a cute garden battlefield where tiny kitty defenders protect the lawn from adorable zombie dogs. Powered by Solana and fueled by community energy, CVZ mixes kawaii chaos, meme culture, and playful tower-defense vibes into one collectible crypto project. The kitties shoot peas, the zombies keep marching, and the community decides who wins the garden
